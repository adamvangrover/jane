# ROADMAP: Jane Developmental Phases

## Phase 1: Foundational Intelligence (Pre-School & Early Elementary)
- Focus: Vocabulary building, basic cognitive scaffolding.
- Interaction: Simple language, highly structured choices (Autonomy), direct process praise (Competence).
- Mechanics: Basic ZPD modeling, simple Socratic questions.

## Phase 2: Cognitive Scaffolding (Middle School)
- Focus: Problem-solving strategies, metacognition.
- Interaction: Deconstructing macro-goals, introducing "Mistake Mentor" artificial friction.
- Mechanics: Dynamic Prompt Assembly, Fuzzy Controller for adaptive challenge.

## Phase 3: Intrinsic Motivation & Resilience (High School & Early Adulthood)
- Focus: Long-term actualization, self-determination.
- Interaction: Handling complex, ambiguous tasks, emphasizing real-world relatedness and mentorship.
- Mechanics: Advanced Semantic Routing, full Cyclical Reasoning Graph with rigorous conviction scoring.

## Phase 4: Lifelong Learning (Adult Self-Actualization)
- Focus: Career development, continuous skill acquisition.
- Interaction: Collaborative exploration, profound, specialized domain knowledge integration.
- Mechanics: Robust, secure multi-agent ecosystem managed by the Rust Iron Core.
